DEFENSE


The first step to fighting cyber crime is to be properly defended. it is important to follow proper cyber hygiene such as account security measures and proper device setup and antivirus. Make sure to never download or run anything that isn't very reputible or open source

Recovery


Recovery starts immediatly after a cyber attack. The volunteer members of Counter Resistance will do everything we can, from nullifying blackmail threats and aecuring accounts to reverse engineering and removing viruses and attempting to decrypt files after a ransomware attack.

Counter Attack


This project is fully in the private sector with no government endorsement. Because of this, we do not directly attack the hackers. Instead we use OSINT and reverse engineering to to gather as much info as possible on them and feed this to the proper athorities to do their part.
